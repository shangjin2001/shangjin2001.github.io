喜欢的科目

Data structure is one of my favorite subjects. First of all, it lays the foundation for many other computer science concepts and algorithms. By understanding data structures such as arrays, linked lists, trees, and graphs, I can better comprehend more complex topics like sorting and searching algorithms.

Secondly, data structure is a highly practical subject. As a cyber security student, I’ve found that many programming projects require an understanding of data structures. By implementing them in real-life scenarios, I can see the direct impact they have on program efficiency and functionality.

Lastly, I find data structure to be a highly stimulating and intellectually challenging subject. The process of designing and implementing data structures involves a great deal of problem-solving and critical thinking. By mastering this subject, I feel like I can take on more complex challenges in the field of computer science.

出于多种原因，数据结构是我最喜欢的科目之一。首先，它为许多其他计算机科学概念和算法奠定了基础。通过理解数组、链表、树和图等数据结构，我可以更好地理解排序和搜索算法等更复杂的主题。

其次，数据结构是一门实践性很强的学科。作为一名网络安全专业的学生，我发现许多编程项目都需要了解数据结构。通过在现实场景中实施它们，我可以看到它们对程序效率和功能的直接影响。

最后，我发现数据结构是一个高度刺激和智力挑战的主题。设计和实现数据结构的过程涉及大量的问题解决和批判性思维。通过掌握这门学科，我觉得我可以在计算机科学领域接受更复杂的挑战。

I am from Cangzhou, Hebei Province. Cangzhou is a coastal city that is known for its rich history and culture. As an important transportation hub in North China, it is easily accessible by high-speed rail, expressway, and air.

Cangzhou has a long history dating back to the Tang Dynasty, and it is home to many historical and cultural sites, such as the Iron Lion of Cangzhou. Overall, Cangzhou is a city with a unique blend of history, culture, and modern industry, making it an attractive destination for visitors and a great place to live and work.

本科专业

My undergraduate major is information security. The essence of information security is to protect information systems and information resources from interference and damage from various threats. In short, it is to protect the security of information. It includes three elements, confidentiality, integrity, Availability, abbreviated as CIA

我的本科专业是信息安全，信息安全的实质是保护信息系统和信息资源免受各种威胁干扰和破坏，简而言之就是保护信息的安全性，它包含三要素，机密性，完整性，可用性，简称为CIA

大数据和普通数据的区别

The storage methods are different, the amount of traditional data is small, while the amount of big data is huge. Data mining methods are different. Traditional data can be mined manually, while big data is generally mined using mining tools. In addition, SQL statements and python and other languages are needed to assist in summarizing and analyzing

存储方式不同，传统数据量小，而大数据数量庞大。数据发掘方式不同，传统数据可以采用人工挖掘，而大数据一般采用挖掘工具进行挖掘，此外还需要借助sql语句和python等语言辅助汇总和分析
